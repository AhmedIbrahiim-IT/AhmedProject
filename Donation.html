Donation:

Your gift will help build healthier futures for all our patients.

Thanks to our wonderful supporters, As-Salam is able to give hope to the thousands of seriously ill people who need our help every year. By setting up a regular gift today you can help make an ongoing difference to the lives of patients and their families at the hospital.

Where your money goes?!

We raise money to enable As-Salam international hospital to provide world-class care for its young patients and their families by:

  • contributing to the rebuilding and refurbishment of the hospital.
  • supporting ground-breaking research into the development of new treatments.
  • funding the most up-to-date equipment.
  • providing accommodation and other support services for patients and their families.
