Visions,Missions&Values

Home | Vision, Missions&Values

Our Vision: To be the most advanced multi-specialty, internationally recognized referral healthcare centre for the Middle East region.

Our Mission: To become the leading multi-specialty tertiary care centre in Egypt within 3 years & the Middle East region within 10 years through a combination of excellent manpower, best medical technology & world class quality systems. In addition, as part of this commitment ASSIH will maintain and continually improve the effectiveness of our Quality Management System.
