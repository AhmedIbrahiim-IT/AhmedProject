Our Nursing Section

Scientists, inventors, doctors, engineers, athletes...... we can all think of famous examples of each of these professions.
The simple truth is that people excel in every field,
but some fields bestow more fame than others.
Nurses are part of one field that remains out of the spotlight,
despite many fine nurses who have made amazing contributions to medicine.
Here are some of the most fame-worthy nurses in history.....

St. Camillus de Lellis he's one of the first male nurses of the profession
Edward Lyon first male nurse to be commissioned in
the Army Nurse Corps in the US as a reserve officer
Joe Hogan story of one male nurse's fight for equal rights

Florence Nightingale the Lady with the Lamp
Virginia Henderson the First Lady of Nursing
Clara Barton the Lady in Charge
