The emergency department provides full-time services around the clock

The emergency department provides full-time services around the clock. It consists of a fully equipped operating room and a recovery room that can reach the patient directly through a special entrance for the ambulance, in addition to two clinics for examination and treatment. This section is overseen by a team of emergency doctors, assisted by a trained nursing team in various types of emergency procedures, including cardiopulmonary resuscitation and cardiac arrest. The department has two fully equipped ambulances to serve and transport patients 24 hours a day.

All hospital rooms are designed and equipped to ensure the safety and safety of patients.

All of the beds are electrically controlled by the patient, equipped with bedside units for medical gases, lighting and call-in. All rooms are air conditioned and have private bathrooms with telephone, TV (satellite TV) and refrigerator.
